	Scene: Select Check Scene

	Date Updated: 6/16/23


----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------


	The Select Check Scene is displayed after the Login Scene. The purpose of this 
	scene is to select whether boards are being checked in or out of the lab.
	Users must select an option provided in the drop-down box. After selecting the 
	correct option, the submit button will bring you to the next scene, which is the 
	Scan Scene.
